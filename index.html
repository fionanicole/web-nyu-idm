The Story of My Name

My first and last name are Irish even though I am only 1/4 Irish. In Gaelic, the name Fiona means fair or just. My last name, Haggerty, however, means unjust or unfair.

...my dad thought he was funny when he came up with that one.

And that was the story of my name: FIONA HAGGERTY
